About Us

We Hate Hiring

But we want to do what we love. As a business, we know that hiring quality UX/UI designers is a painful process full of misinformation and risk. But as creatives that live to wake up each morning and building something amazing, we know that potential employees spend their days trying to find the opportunity to fuel their passion and end up with a lot of doubt, hopelessness, and even the occasional exploitation.

Dapper wants to fix hiring. We're starting with UI/UX because its an area we know and are passionate about. No more recruiters hounding employers with all noise and no signal. No more endless email holding patterns, waiting to see if Schrodinger's job offer will ever materialize. We take a stand, and we start here.

We're here to make sure everyone wins.
